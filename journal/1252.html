Friendship in Atlas Shrugged

by Peter Saint-Andre

2008-07-26

My friend Kurt Leininger recently clued me in to the fact that William Thomas, writing in The New Individualist, had some positive to things to say about my essay "Friendship in Atlas Shrugged" (which I contributed to the volume Atlas Shrugged: A Philosophical and Literary Companion). So I figure this is a good time to post it online (in the public domain, naturally). Enjoy!

And while I'm posting Randiana, I suppose I'll also upload Ayn Rand, Novelist, an essay-review of The Literary Art of Ayn Rand that was published in the Fall 2007 issue of Journal of Ayn Rand Studies. Now if only I could find the time to write my long-promised essay on Rand and Aristotle, I'd be just about done with my reflections on Ayn Rand. Maybe in 2009. :)
