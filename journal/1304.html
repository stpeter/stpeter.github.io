Got Password?

by Peter Saint-Andre

2009-01-14

Because I'm an admin of the jabber.org IM service, I can change people's passwords -- in fact I'm the official contact for such requests. What never ceases to amaze me is that I receive such requests from plenty of people whose IM accounts are not hosted at jabber.org, but instead at Google Talk, companies like FedEx and HP, even NASA. But just now I received a call that tops them all: someone at the Defense Information Systems Agency asked me if I could change their password. Um, no, I don't think I have access to the SIPRNet. :)
