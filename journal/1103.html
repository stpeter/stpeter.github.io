OpenToken

2007-07-03

My friend Dave Smith and I just submitted to the IETF the first version of a specification for OpenToken, an open format for the secure, cross-application exchange of key-value pairs. We met IRL today to discuss some improvements (better explain the context, provide more examples, etc.), so we'll probably submit the -01 version soonish. Feedback is welcome as always. :)
