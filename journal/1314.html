Anarchitecture

by Peter Saint-Andre

2009-02-26

I had to laugh the other day when someone who's quite active in the Jabber/XMPP developer community privately praised my leadership abilities. Me, a leader? Not really. My preferred word for my philosophy of community building is anarchitecture, which I'd describe as "how to lead without leading". One of these years I'll have to write an essay on the topic...
