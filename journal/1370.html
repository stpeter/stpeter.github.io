Sobering Thoughts

2009-07-13

For some sobering thoughts on what's in store with regard to the economy and society, I highly recommend the following essays that came across my radar today:

  • Debt and Deflation by Van R. Hoisington and Lacy H. Hunt
  • Buddy, Can You Spare $5 Trillion? by John Mauldin
  • A 20-Year Bear Market? by David Galland

Grokking all three in fullness will drive any notions of "green shoots" right out of your head.
