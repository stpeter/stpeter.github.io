Monadnock Valley Press Annual Report 2022

by Peter Saint-Andre

2022-12-26

This was a big year for uploading public-domain works to the Monadnock Valley Press - likely the biggest year since I founded the site in 2008. Indeed, I republished works by 50 authors: Dante Alighieri, Aristotle, Matthew Arnold, Jane Austen, John Burroughs, Thomas Campion, Willa Cather, Joseph Conrad, William Cowper, Frederick Douglass, John Dryden, George Eliot, E.M. Forster, Robert Herrick, Hesiod, Robert Hillyer, Langston Hughes, David Hume, Henrik Ibsen, William James, Robinson Jeffers, Ben Jonson, Emma Lazarus, Henry Wadsworth Longfellow, Lucretius, Christopher Marlowe, Herman Melville, John Stuart Mill, John Milton, Michel de Montaigne, Kakuzo Okakura, Richard Overton, Walter Pater, Plutarch, Sir Walter Raleigh, Laura Riding, Edwin Arlington Robinson, Bertrand Russell, William Shakespeare, Sophocles, Baruch Spinoza, Wallace Stevens, Sara Teasdale, Henry David Thoreau, Leo Tolstoy, Virgil, Voltaire, George Washington, John Greenleaf Whittier, and Xenophon. Whew!

Although I make no promises for 2023 because I have several large projects underway, I expect to republish a number of additional works, albeit perhaps at a slower pace.
