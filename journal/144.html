Overcoming Left and Right

2001-11-20

Ari Armstrong has put together a web page containing reflections on the September 11 atrocity from various individuals who are broadly within the libertarian tradition. Interestingly, the perspectives range from rabidly pro-war (e.g., hard-core Randian Leonard Peikoff) to rabidly anti-war (e.g., long-time anarchist and two-time LP presidential candidate Harry Browne). Does libertarianism truly overcome the supposedly specious distinctions between left and right? Sometimes I wonder....
