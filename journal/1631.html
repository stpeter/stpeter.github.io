Hummus Recipe

by Peter Saint-Andre

2019-07-21

One of my favorite things to eat in the summer (and in truth all year round) is hummus. Because multiple people have requested my recipe over the years, I figured I'd share it here.

Here are the ingredients:

  • 15 oz garbanzo beans
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • pinch cayenne
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1/8 cup water

Place all ingredients in a food processor and process for 10-15 seconds. That's it!

I like my hummus thick, garlicky, and chunky - if your preferences differ, increase the water, cut back on the garlic, and increase the processing time.

Enjoy!
