Huzzah!

by Peter Saint-Andre

2011-03-30

I'm happy to announce that the RFC Editor has just published RFC 6120, RFC 6121, RFC 6122, and, for good measure, RFC 6125. These specifications define the fundamentals of XMPP and are a big improvement over the old RFCs that we finished in 2004. Thanks to everyone who helped me get these documents across the finish line!
