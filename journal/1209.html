The Road From Damascus

by Peter Saint-Andre

2008-02-13

Did we really need to declare war on Iraq or even Afghanistan in order take out the criminals who perpetrated the 9/11 attacks and other acts of terror? I wonder. Today came news that psychopath Imad Mughniyeh of Hizbullah has been killed by a car bomb in Damascus. Yes, Damascus -- capital city of Syria, supposed member of the axis of evil, and a land eminently not occupied by the armed forces of the U.S. government. As an Israeli commando says in this Jane's article, "To fight these bastards you don’t need a military attack. You only need to adopt Israel’s assassination policy."
