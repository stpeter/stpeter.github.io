Monadnock Update

2008-02-13

I've been working some more on monadnock.net. I really like the idea of publishing public domain texts of significance -- exercising taste and judgment in selecting important works to make available for free in easy-to-read formats. After the initial uploads, I recently posted the Essays: First Series by Ralph Waldo Emerson and some poems by Andrew Marvell. Maybe I'll need to create a blog at monadnock.net to announce these site updates... :)
