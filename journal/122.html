Victimless Crimes

by Peter Saint-Andre

2001-10-25

The Uniform Crime Report is out for the year 2000. 1.6 million arrests were made for drug offenses, more than for murder, rape, arson, aggravated assault, robbery, burglary, and auto theft combined. 81 percent of drug arrests were for personal possession -- a victimless "crime". Isn't it time to end the war on drugs?
