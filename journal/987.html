LP Reform?

by Peter Saint-Andre

2006-07-30

Last week I posted some Whiggish thoughts about the prospects for freedom in America. It seems that Denverite David Aitken recently participated in the national convention of the Libertarian Party, where as a member of the Libertarian Reform Caucus he had a hand in producing the LP's new platform. Is there hope for the LP? I'm not yet sure, but I may take another look.
