Simplicity

2011-12-07

Six months ago or so I found myself inspired by reading Edward Tufte, as a result of which I simplified my blog. But it's still too busy. It needs to be much simpler -- like inessential.com by Brent Simmons, which is just gorgeous to me and looks fantastic on mobile devices. Brent uses custom (but unreleased) software, so I might need to develop my own approach as well (something even simpler than the old system I ditched back in 2007). Simplify, simplify, simplify.
