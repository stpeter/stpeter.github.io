Blogito Ergo Sum

by Peter Saint-Andre

2001-10-08

Dave Winer says that "journalism is a person writing a journal". I like that. I guess that makes any blogger into a journalist -- and often the amateurs (in the original sense of the word, of course) are more worth reading than the so-called professionals.

Blogito ergo sum -- I blog, therefore I am. :)
