Ayn Rand, Novelist

2008-03-06

The other day I received the latest issue of The Journal of Ayn Rand Studies, containing my brief essay-review of The Literary Art of Ayn Rand (a compilation of essays on Rand as novelist). Sometime in October or so I'll post it online here.

One interesting paper in this issue is "A Critique of Rand's Theory of Intellectual Property Rights" by Timothy Sandefur. If I can find the time I'll be submitting a reply to that one, since I'm rather interested in the topic...
