Randian Notes

by Peter Saint-Andre

2006-09-12

Herewith an update about various further essays of mine on Ayn Rand:

  • It's been over three months since the Journal of Ayn Rand Studies published my essay Image and Integration in Ayn Rand's Descriptive Style in Volume 7 Number 2, so I've posted it online under my usual public domain licensing.

  • My essay "Friendship in Atlas Shrugged" is forthcoming in Ayn Rand's Atlas Shrugged: A Philosophical and Literary Companion, due out in 2007 from Ashgate Publishing.

  • Chris Sciabarra has informed me that my essay "Nietzsche, Rand, and the Ethics of the Great Task" has been accepted for publication in a forthcoming special issue of the Journal of Ayn Rand Studies on Nietzsche and Rand (due out in 2007, I think).

Now I just need to write my projected essay on Aristotle and Rand, which I think will be my last rung on the ladder.
