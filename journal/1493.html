Ancient Fire

by Peter Saint-Andre

2013-09-02

This self-publishing stuff is fun! Over the last few months I've published both The Tao of Roark and Letters on Happiness. Recently I added another book to the list: Ancient Fire, a collection of poems and translations that I made in the 1990s. Despite the fact that I don't claim to be more than a second- or third-rank poet, I've received positive feedback over the years about several of the poems in this collection so I figured it would be good to make them available in print and ebook formats. Next on my list: The Ism Book (which I originally wrote in 1996 and have revised several times since then).

Oh, and if you enjoy any of these books, please consider writing a review on Amazon so that other folks can find them, too. Thanks. :-)
