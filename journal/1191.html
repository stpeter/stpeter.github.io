Jacob Bronowski

by Peter Saint-Andre

2008-01-18

Timothy Sandefur just clued me in to the fact that today is the 100th anniversary of the birth of Jacob Bronowski. I have long admired Bronowski for his intellectual honesty and fearless independence, and I highly recommend his works for anyone who is interested in philosophy, history, biology, psychology, the sciences, and the arts.
