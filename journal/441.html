micropiece

by Peter Saint-Andre

2003-09-28

Thanks to Claire Wolfe, I just found an amazing piece of writing by a late-shift cashier at a truckstop in Mississippi. (Or so he says: perhaps that's just a persona, and the essay is pure fiction; but if it is, it's damn good fiction.) Most folks wouldn't expect much from any person in such a position. Most folks would be wrong.
