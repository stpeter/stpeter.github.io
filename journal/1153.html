An Odometer Reading

2007-11-01

This evening as I pulled into the alley behind my house, my car's trip meter (which I zeroed out on January 1st and haven't touched since) rolled over to 2000 miles. So even though I drive a gas-guzzling Isuzu Trooper, I've driven it only 200 miles a month so far this year...
