A Philosophy for Living on Earth

by Peter Saint-Andre

2007-10-29

Over the last few weeks I have copy-edited (and in a few cases more radically modified) all the essays in my collection A Philosophy for Living on Earth: Essays on Ayn Rand and Objectivism. I still need to complete the essay on Aristotle and Rand, then I will be finished with this book. Perhaps I'll even self-publish it on (gasp!) paper in a few years' time. In the meantime it's all available for free on the 'net. Comments are welcome as always. :)
