Words4Nerds #10

by Peter Saint-Andre

2002-10-24

This week's words all describe colors:

  • viridescent -- green
  • xanthous -- yellow
  • cerulean -- sky-blue (from Latin caelo for the heavens)
  • amaranthine -- deep or reddish purple
  • vinaceous -- wine-red

Have a vinaceous weekend!
