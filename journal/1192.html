Monadnock Repurposed

by Peter Saint-Andre

2008-01-18

Back in 1997 I started publishing a webzine entitled the Monadnock Review. I stopped publishing the review in 2002, but I've long wanted to again do something productive with the monadnock.net domain. In the last few weeks I've done just that by starting to republish public-domain texts there (Ã  la my old idea of a public domain press). So far I've republished three texts: Anthem by Ayn Rand, Civil Disobedience by Henry David Thoreau, and The Antichrist by Friedrich Nietzsche. I'll be publishing many more public-domain texts as I find the time to do so.
