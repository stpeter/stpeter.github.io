Some Goals for 2014

by Peter Saint-Andre

2013-12-17

Here are some of my goals for 2014:

  • Complete all of the specifications I'm currently working on in the XMPP, STOX, PRECIS, UTA, and URNBIS working groups at the IETF.
  • Resist taking on any new IETF-related tasks for awhile. :-)
  • Form a cooperative to provide an organizational structure (and funding source) for the jabber.org IM service.
  • Finish translating everything that Epicurus said on the nature of the good life, and publish the results as a short book.
  • Write more code, especially on projects related to private communications and Internet security.
  • Play more music, and record more of what I play.
  • Spend more time with friends and family.
  • Get outside more often.
  • Gain more wisdom.
  • Breathe more freely.
  • Enjoy life more fully.
  • Thrive.
