The Anglosphere Challenge

by Peter Saint-Andre

2004-11-18

Thanks to a tip from Survival Arts, I've just discovered that Jim Bennett's book on the Anglosphere has been published. The table of contents looks positively mouth-watering if you go in for civilizational history and large-scale but well-grounded speculation about the near future. I don't buy many books, but I may run out and purchase this one! (Bennett has also started an Anglosphere Institute, it seems.)
