Randian Reflections

2009-02-22

Yesterday I translated some passages from Aristotle's Nicomachean Ethics for my essay Our Man in Greece, so I finally declare my book Randian Reflections to be done. One of the essays (on Nietzsche and Rand) is still to be published in the Journal of Ayn Rand Studies, but the others are complete enough that I am going to leave them behind now. Perhaps if I ever print them in a paper volume I'll revisit them all (the political essays are especially weak, I feel), but that day will arrive some years in the future (if ever) and it's time to move on so that I can finish The Tao of Roark (originally begun ten years ago) and my Epicurus translations. More about those in the coming months...
