One Small Voice

2003-08-31

Since I launched this weblog, its title has been the same as the nickname I use on Jabber: "stpeter". After two years, I've decided to finally give it an official name. The name I've chosen is "one small voice", which is in part an ironic reference to Ellsworth Toohey's New York Banner column in Ayn Rand's novel The Fountainhead, and in part a serious attempt to capture the fact that I speak only for myself, as well as to honor the most important minority of all: the individual. It's all about the power of one.
