[Oxy]moronic

by Peter Saint-Andre

2008-06-02

Here in Colorado, Wash Park Prophet opines about a new U.S. government report on climate change that reportedly has been "hailed for not ignoring mainstream science as previous reports did".

I'm sorry, but science is not a matter of counting noses, it is a matter of tracking reality. Therefore the term "mainstream science" is worse than oxymoronic -- it is simply moronic.
