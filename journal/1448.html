RAZ: Songs of Bob Dylan

2012-02-18

This evening I broke out my jumbo Taylor six-string and played some Bob Dylan songs: The Times They Are A-Changin', Honey Just Allow Me One More Chance, Corinna, Corinna, It's All Right Ma (I'm Only Bleeding), and a few of my other favorites. I've long wanted to record a collection of Dylan songs, which I'd entitle "RAZ" (the initials of his given name, Robert Allen Zimmerman). Maybe I can work on that one after I finish my Squire Variations...
