The Bard Unleashed

2006-11-14

NewsForge reports on Open Shakespeare, whose objective is to provide the complete works of Shakespeare in a completely open form. Wait, you ask, isn't all of Shakespeare in the public domain given that he wrote his plays and poems around 400 years ago? Well, not necessarily. The Bard certainly deserves to be open knowledge and the Open Knowledge Foundation is working to make it happen. Kudos!
