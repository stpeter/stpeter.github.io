Suggestions for Further Exploration

If you enjoyed The Tao of Roark, here are some other works you might like:

  • The Fountainhead by Ayn Rand
  • Tao Te Ching by Lao Tzu (my preferred translation is by Derek Lin)
  • The Gnostic Gospels by Elaine Pagels
  • Old Nick's Guide to Happiness by Nicholas Dykes
  • The Joyful Wisdom by Friedrich Nietzsche
  • Goldberg Variations by J.S. Bach (my favorite recordings are Glenn Gould's 1981 version and a transcription for harp by Catrin Finch)

The Tao of Roark is the first volume in a six-movement suite of books I'm writing on the art of living:

  1. The Tao of Roark: Variations on a Theme from Ayn Rand (2012)
  2. Songs of Zarathustra: Poetic Perspectives on Nietzsche's Philosophy of Life (2018)
  3. The Upland Farm: Thoreau on Cultivating a Better Life (2017)
  4. Letters on Happiness: An Epicurean Dialogue (2013)
  5. Gods Among Men: A Novel of Pyrrho and Alexander the Great (forthcoming)
  6. Complete Thyself: Aristotle on Human Fulfillment (forthcoming)
