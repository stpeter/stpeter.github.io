An Aristotelian Glossary

Last Updated: 2023-11-20

  • Activity translates energeia (ἐνέργεια). This is perhaps the most important term in Aristotle's account of human action. Its core meaning is being engaged in working at a Task, which is why Aristotle scholar Joe Sachs rendered it as "being-at-work". Aristotle repeatedly points out that it's not good enough to be Thriving in your character and thinking, if that means merely having a Trait but not using and applying it in your day-to-day activities (after all, even someone who is asleep can have a trait). Life simply is activity.

  • Aliveness translates psuchē (ψυχή). Although psuchē was traditionally translated as "soul", for Aristotle it's what animates a living being, what makes it a living thing - i.e., its aliveness. Even plants have psuchē!

  • Awareness translates theōria (θεωρία). At root, the Activity that separates animals and humans from plants and inanimate objects is their keen awareness of the world around them.

  • Balance translates mesotēs (μεσότης).

  • Beautifully Right translates kalos (καλός).

  • Beneficial translates agathos (ἀγαθός).

  • Capacity translates dunamis (δύναμις).

  • Character translates ēthos (ἦθος).

  • Commitment translates prohairesis (προαίρεσις).

  • Complete translates teleia (τελεία).

  • Completion translates entelecheia (ἐντελέχεια).

  • Correct translates orthos (ὀρθός).

  • Corruption translates mochthēria (μοχθηρία).

  • Craft translates technē (τέχνη).

  • Enjoyment translates hēdonē (ἡδονή). Every kind of Activity has its own Innate kind of enjoyment: the joys of eating, say, are quite different from the joys of beholding works of art. This is why the Standard of what's truly enjoyable is the Serious person of Complete Thriving in both Character and Thinking.

  • Fitting translates prepon (πρέπον).

  • Fulfillment translates eudaimonia (εὐδαιμονία).

  • Goal translates telos (τέλος).

  • Harmful translates kakos (κακός).

  • Humaneness translates epieikeia (ἐπιείκεια).

  • Identity translates to ti ēn einai (τὸ τί ἦν εἶναι).

  • Impulsiveness translates akrasia (ἀκρασία).

  • Innate translates oikeios (οἰκεῖος).

  • Insight translates nous (νοῦς).

  • Life translates zōē (ζωή).

  • Love translates philia (φιλία).

  • Moderation translates sōphrosunē (σωφροσύνη).

  • Reaching translates orexis (ὄρεξις).

  • Reaction translates pathos (πάθος).

  • Reflect translates bouleuesthai (βουλεύεσθαι).

  • Resolution translates boulēsis (βούλησις).

  • Sagacity translates sophia (σοφία).

  • Self-Indulgence translates akolasia (ἀκολασία).

  • Self-Restraint translates enkrateia (ἐγκράτεια).

  • Serious translates spoudaios (σπουδαῖος).

  • Social translates politikē (πολιτική).

  • Standard translates horos (ὅρος).

  • Taking Action translates praxis (πρᾶξις).

  • Task translates ergon (ἔργον).

  • Thinghood translates ousia (οὐσία).

  • Thinking translates logos (λόγος).

  • Thriving translates aretē (ἀρετή).

  • Trait translates hexis (ἕξις).

  • Understanding translates epistēmē (ἐπιστήμη).

  • Valuable translates haireta (αἱρετά).

  • Want translates thumos (θυμός).

  • Way of Being translates eidos (εἶδος).

  • Way of Life translates bios (βίος).

  • Wholeness translates autarkeia (αὐτάρκεια).

  • Wisdom translates phronēsis (φρόνησις).
