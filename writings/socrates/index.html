No One Wiser

An Intellectual Biography of Socrates

by Peter Saint-Andre

After I finish writing books about Aristotle and Pyrrho, I'd like to create a brief biography of Socrates.

At the moment I am thinking that the book will examine Socrates' life and thought through a consideration of Plato's Socratic dialogues in dramatic order, as well as modern reflections on his relevance to a life well lived. I might even compose the whole thing as a dialogue (perhaps between Plato and Aristotle!), but I have yet to settle on the format.

As always with projects of this sort, I'm keeping notes and maintaining a reading list.
