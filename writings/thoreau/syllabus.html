Syllabus: Thoreau Through the Year

by Peter Saint-Andre

What follows is the draft of a syllabus for a monthly reading group devoted to Henry David Thoreau.

March

  • Walden: Economy
  • Seasons of Thoreau: March
  • The Upland Farm: Farm
  • The Upland Farm: Seed
  • Poem: "Forever in My Dream and in My Morning Thought"
  • Poem: "Rumors from an Aeolian Harp"
  • Poem: "The Old Marlborough Road"

April

  • Walden: Where I Lived, and What I Lived For
  • Seasons of Thoreau: April
  • The Upland Farm: Preparing
  • The Upland Farm: Sprout
  • Essay: "Walking"
  • Poem: "Prayer"
  • Poem: "It Is a Real Place"

May

  • Walden: Reading
  • Walden: Sounds
  • Seasons of Thoreau: May
  • The Upland Farm: Planting
  • The Upland Farm: Tree
  • The Upland Farm: Trust
  • Essay: "The Succession of Forest Trees"
  • Poem: "Nature"

June

  • Walden: Solitude
  • Walden: Visitors
  • Seasons of Thoreau: June
  • The Upland Farm: Flower
  • Poem: "Sic Vita"
  • Poem: "The Summer Rain"
  • Poem: "Great Friend"

July

  • Walden: The Bean-Field
  • Seasons of Thoreau: July
  • The Upland Farm: Cultivating
  • Essay: "A Walk to Wachusett"
  • Poem: "Not Unconcerned"
  • Poem: "The Mountains in the Horizon"

August

  • Walden: The Village
  • Walden: The Ponds
  • Seasons of Thoreau: August
  • The Upland Farm: Magnanimity
  • Poem: "Friendship"
  • Poem: "It Is No Dream of Mine"

September

  • Walden: Baker Farm
  • Seasons of Thoreau: September
  • The Upland Farm: Fruit
  • The Upland Farm: Harvesting
  • Essay: "Life Wthout Principle"
  • Poem: "Sympathy"
  • Poem: "Conscience is Instinct Bred in the Bone"

October

  • Walden: Higher Laws
  • Seasons of Thoreau: October
  • The Upland Farm: Independence
  • The Upland Farm: Leaf
  • Essay: "Autumnal Tints"
  • Poem: "Independence"
  • Poem: "Manhood"
  • Poem: "I Am the Autumnal Sun"
  • Poem: "On Fields O'er Which the Reaper's Hand Has Passed"

November

  • Walden: Brute Neighbors
  • Walden: House Warming
  • Seasons of Thoreau: November
  • The Upland Farm: Storing
  • Essay: "Wild Apples"
  • Poem: "Music"

December

  • Walden: Former Inhabitants and Winter Visitors
  • Walden: Winter Animals
  • Seasons of Thoreau: December
  • The Upland Farm: Simplicity
  • Poem: "At Midnight's Hour I Raised My Head"
  • Poem: "Within the Circuit of This Plodding Life"

January

  • Walden: The Pond in Winter
  • Seasons of Thoreau: January
  • The Upland Farm: Bud
  • Essay: "A Winter Walk"
  • Poem: "I Do Not Fear My Thoughts Will Die"

February

  • Walden: Spring
  • Walden: Conclusion
  • Seasons of Thoreau: February
  • The Upland Farm: Offering
  • Poem: "To a Marsh Hawk in Spring"
  • Poem: "When in Some Cove I Lie"
  • Poem: "My Life Has Been the Poem"
