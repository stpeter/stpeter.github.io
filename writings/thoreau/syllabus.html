Syllabus: Thoreau Through the Year

by Peter Saint-Andre

What follows is the draft of a syllabus for a monthly reading group devoted to Henry David Thoreau.

March:

  • Walden: Economy
  • Seasons of Thoreau: March
  • The Upland Farm: Farm
  • The Upland Farm: Seed
  • "Rumors from an Aeolian Harp"
  • "The Old Marlborough Road"

April:

  • Walden: Where I Lived, and What I Lived For
  • Seasons of Thoreau: April
  • The Upland Farm: Preparing
  • The Upland Farm: Sprout
  • "Walking"
  • "Prayer"

May:

  • Walden: Reading
  • Walden: Sounds
  • Seasons of Thoreau: May
  • The Upland Farm: Planting
  • The Upland Farm: Tree
  • The Upland Farm: Trust
  • "The Succession of Forest Trees"
  • "Nature"

June:

  • Walden: Solitude
  • Walden: Visitors
  • Seasons of Thoreau: June
  • The Upland Farm: Flower
  • "Sic Vita"
  • "The Summer Rain"

July:

  • Walden: The Bean-Field
  • Seasons of Thoreau: July
  • The Upland Farm: Cultivating
  • "A Walk to Wachusett"
  • "Not Unconcerned"
  • "The Mountains in the Horizon"

August:

  • Walden: The Village
  • Walden: The Ponds
  • Seasons of Thoreau: August
  • The Upland Farm: Magnanimity
  • "Friendship"
  • "It Is No Dream of Mine"

September:

  • Walden: Baker Farm
  • Seasons of Thoreau: September
  • The Upland Farm: Fruit
  • The Upland Farm: Harvesting
  • "Life Wthout Principle"
  • "Sympathy"
  • "Conscience is Instinct Bred in the Bone"

October:

  • Walden: Higher Laws
  • Seasons of Thoreau: October
  • The Upland Farm: Independence
  • The Upland Farm: Leaf
  • "Autumnal Tints"
  • "Independence"
  • "I Am the Autumnal Sun"

November:

  • Walden: Brute Neighbors
  • Walden: House Warming
  • Seasons of Thoreau: November
  • The Upland Farm: Storing
  • "Wild Apples"

December:

  • Walden: Former Inhabitants and Winter Visitors
  • Walden: Winter Animals
  • Seasons of Thoreau: December
  • The Upland Farm: Simplicity
  • "Within the Circuit of This Plodding Life"

January:

  • Walden: The Pond in Winter
  • Seasons of Thoreau: January
  • The Upland Farm: Bud
  • "A Winter Walk"
  • "I Do Not Fear My Thoughts Will Die"

February:

  • Walden: Spring
  • Walden: Conclusion
  • Seasons of Thoreau: February
  • The Upland Farm: Offering
  • "When in Some Cove I Lie"
  • "My Life Has Been the Poem"
