Suggestions for Further Exploration

If you enjoyed The Upland Farm, here are some other works you might like:

  • Seasons of Thoreau, a companion volume of selections from Thoreau's writings
  • Thoreau's Living Ethics: Walden and the Pursuit of Virtue by Philip Cafaro
  • The Gita within Walden by Paul Friedrich
  • Henry Thoreau: A Life of the Mind by Robert D. Richardson
  • Thoreau: The Quest and the Classics by Ethel Seybold

The Upland Farm is the fourth volume in a six-movement suite of books I'm writing on the art of living:

  1. The Tao of Roark: Variations on a Theme from Ayn Rand (2012)
  2. Songs of Zarathustra: Poetic Perspectives on Nietzsche's Philosophy of Life (2018)
  3. The Upland Farm: Thoreau on Cultivating a Better Life (2017)
  4. Letters on Happiness: An Epicurean Dialogue (2013)
  5. Gods Among Men: A Novel of Pyrrho and Alexander the Great (forthcoming)
  6. Complete Thyself: Aristotle on Human Fulfillment (forthcoming)
