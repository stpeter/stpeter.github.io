The Skeptic Speaks

by Peter Saint-Andre

Hold off, delay, suspend assent
To moral rules and wisdom claims
From dogmatists of every bent:
Make thought and choice your highest aims.

Take time and counsel in your mind
Before you reach your own conclusions
In matters of the finest kind —
And thus steer clear of life's delusions.
