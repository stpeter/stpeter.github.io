Second Benediction

by Peter Saint-Andre

Go down to do the work for which you're meant
And make a meaning more than happiness;
But know that through the meaning of your task
You make yourself one of the gods who bless.

(cf. Thus Spoke Zarathustra, "The Sign"; The Joyful Learning, ยง125)
