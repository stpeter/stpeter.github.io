Songs of Zarathustra

Poetic Perspectives on Nietzsche's Philosophy of Life

by Peter Saint-Andre

Although Friedrich Nietzsche is best known for his thunderous pronouncements about the death of god and the eternal recurrence, hidden within his writings is also a wealth of practical wisdom. Songs of Zarathustra condenses the relevant ideas into a cycle of poems, including new translations of some of Nietzsche's own verse. Ordered chronologically from The Birth of Tragedy (1872) through Nietzsche's final works (composed in 1888), these poems track his growth as a thinker. Above all, Songs of Zarathustra presents a fresh, positive perspective on Nietzsche's philosophy and its applications to daily living.
