Benediction

by Peter Saint-Andre

Go soar so far above the things you love
That you can view them from a godly height;
But know that gods as yet unknown go down
To bathe the human world in earthly light.

(cf. Thus Spoke Zarathustra, "Zarathustra's Prologue")
