I'm Peter Saint-Andre, a.k.a. stpeter.

Although for many years I worked full-time as a technologist, I'm now focused on writing, training, coaching, and music.

As a writer, I've published half a dozen books, including encounters with Nietzsche, Thoreau, Epicurus, and Rand. My next book will be an epitome of Aristotle's views on human flourishing.

As a trainer, I am working with my wife Elisa George to create a professional development seminar that applies insights from positive psychology, organizational leadership, and classical philosophy to work and career.

As a coach and startup advisor, I have developed a dialectical method for philosophical coaching and I advise clients at all stages of life and career to help them truly flourish.

As a musician, I have composed and arranged numerous songs for voice and guitar as well as extended pieces for classical guitar and electric bass. My current focus is a transcription of the Bach Cello Suites for solo bass.

My online journal is updated weekly. Everything I work on is placed into the public domain and available under source control. I also republish judiciously selected public domain texts via the Monadnock Valley Press. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to have a conversation.
