Aubade

These extended works have their origins in my simple love for the guitar. Although I listen to piano music more than I listen to guitar music (I like to say that I have "pianist envy"), I have always loved the feel and the sound of the guitar above all else. I wrote most of these pieces long ago, indeed I started noodling with some of these themes as far back as my teenage years. Although I have not yet recorded them in any form (and I plan to record my Yes transcriptions first), you can find sheet music below.

  • Passion Suite (1988)
  • River Suite (1990)
  • Arctic Suite (1993)
  • Troubadour Variations (2002)
