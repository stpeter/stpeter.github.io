Peter Saint-Andre: Music

I've been playing guitar and bass, singing songs, and writing music for a long time. The music I create comes in three varieties under numerous influences: acoustic singer-songwriter fare, extended pieces for solo classical guitar with new-age overtones, and arrangements for solo electric bass.

I'm currently working on four projects, focusing mostly on the first one for now:

  • Fundamental Affirmation — arrangements for solo bass of songs by the progressive rock band Yes
  • Aubade — three suites and a set of variations for solo classical guitar
  • Freedom's Plow — a musical setting of Langston Hughes's long poem about America and freedom
  • Hagiography — a suite of original songs about life and love

Eventually I hope to also work on solo bass recordings of music by Rush, Duke Ellington and of Bach's Cello Suites, and to perhaps make a recording or two of covers.
